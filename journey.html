My Story

I was an average student in my school days and did not expect my career and life to connect with Information Technology. I do not have a tech background, but I make great efforts to succeed in my tech journey. I am the first graduate in my family's generation and am pursuing an MSC degree. I started my journey in full-stack development when I completed my Higher National Diploma in 2023, and I did my BEng (Hons) in Software Engineering with First Class Honors in 2024 where I believed in my hard work and tons of effort. The research which I did in my Higher National Diploma increased my curiosity in the research. In 2023 I did research on the Impact of Green IoT on Carbon Footprint as a case of digital transformation; Special case to Data Center Consumers in Sri Lanka (Eastern Province). However, the analysis part of the research and derived answers and predictions through the data analysis increased my interest in working with datasets which helped to divert my tech journey into the data science field.

In 2024, I started my MSc journey after my degree graduation without any further delay and without having experience in the data science field. Now, I am 24 years old and pursuing an MSc it is quite interesting but on the other hand, without experience surviving with experienced students in the MSc journey is quite harder. But the willingness to self-learn with god grace pushed me to take part in this battle and prove myself. Let me say, that trusting yourself to achieve your destination never makes you fail therefore, the path is harder, but I trust the process and every day I learn new things and shape my life to success in the journey.
